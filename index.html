What you see before you is a memorial ship. We are what’s left of a life of travel between the shores. We are full of rust. Our anchor is down. You have permission to see between the—walk between the cracks.

We are complicated stillness.

We are stormy Tara Sophie Izzy Ella Aye Leanna and Ann-Marie. Or versions of them. Or a fraction of who we were before. We’re unsure of what is next.

We are melodrama trauma freaks who walk like riverboats on fire.

The beer is to your right.

- Riverboat, September 2017 viewing guide
